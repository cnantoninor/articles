Epistemic Debt Formula:

Ed = Sigma_k integral_0^T (Cs,k(t) - Gc,k(t)) dt

Where:

  • Cs,k(t) = system complexity at layer k
  • Gc,k(t) = cognitive grasp at layer k
  • k indexes: L4 (requirements), L3 (architecture), L2 (design), L1 (implementation)

Net benefit condition:

Net benefit = delta (AI speed) - Sigma_k c_k * tau_k (recovery costs)

When recovery costs exceed speed gains, epistemic debt makes AI-assisted development a net negative.

Recovery cost multipliers (Boehm):

  • L4 (Requirements): 30-70x
  • L3 (Architecture): ~10x
  • L2 (Design): 3-6x
  • L1 (Implementation): ~1x
